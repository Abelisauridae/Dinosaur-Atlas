# Sample Family Prompts

These are first-pass production prompts for the five approved test families.

## Abelisauridae

```text
Create a painterly natural-history paleoart illustration of Abelisauridae, a dinosaur family from the Dinosaur Atlas project. Show one featured adult abelisaurid in a full-body hero composition, landscape format, with the entire head, torso, limbs, and tail visible. Use a low-to-mid camera angle and a strong 3/4 profile. Emphasize the family-defining anatomy: deep skull, short horned face or cranial ornamentation, tiny forelimbs, powerful hind legs, muscular tail, compact predatory build. The silhouette must read instantly at thumbnail size.

Visual style: cinematic painterly realism, museum-quality paleoart, rich skin texture, believable musculature, controlled atmospheric perspective, warm natural light, dramatic but plausible prehistoric environment, detailed but not chaotic background, no fantasy exaggeration.

Environment: semi-arid floodplain with distant rocky uplands and subtle volcanic geology, but keep the dinosaur as the clear focal point. Lighting: warm late-afternoon light from one side with soft haze. Color direction: charcoal, umber, rust, and muted ember-orange accents. Mood: powerful, predatory, grounded, and elegant rather than monstrous.
```

## Ceratopsidae

```text
Create a painterly natural-history paleoart illustration of Ceratopsidae, a dinosaur family from the Dinosaur Atlas project. Show one featured adult ceratopsid in a full-body hero composition, landscape format, with the entire head, frill, limbs, and tail visible. Use a low-to-mid camera angle and a strong 3/4 profile. Emphasize the family-defining anatomy: large facial frill, prominent brow horns, nasal horn, heavy quadrupedal body, beaked mouth, rugged skin texture. The silhouette must read instantly at thumbnail size.

Visual style: cinematic painterly realism, museum-quality paleoart, rich surface detail, believable weight, controlled atmospheric perspective, warm natural light, detailed but restrained prehistoric habitat.

Environment: open woodland edge with dusty ground, scattered conifers, and distant sunlit haze. Lighting: warm directional morning light with soft back haze. Color direction: ochre, sandstone, bark-brown, muted olive, and horn-gray. Mood: formidable, ancient, noble, and grounded.
```

## Hadrosauridae

```text

Color direction: moss green, muted tan, olive, cream, and soft earth-brown. Mood: serene, majestic, and biologically plausible.
```


## Mamenchisauridae

```text
Create a painterly natural-history paleoart illustration of Mamenchisauridae, a dinosaur family from the Dinosaur Atlas project. Show one featured adult mamenchisaurid in a full-body hero composition, landscape format, with the entire head, extremely long neck, torso, limbs, and tail visible. Use a low-to-mid camera angle and a strong side or 3/4 profile that keeps the entire neck readable. Emphasize the family-defining anatomy: extraordinarily long neck, small head, columnar limbs, deep torso, long balancing tail, immense but graceful sauropod proportions. The silhouette must read instantly at thumbnail size.

Visual style: cinematic painterly realism, museum-quality paleoart, believable scale, warm atmospheric light, layered landscape depth, elegant painterly finish.

Environment: broad floodplain or mountain valley with open space so the neck and scale can be appreciated without clutter. Lighting: warm late-afternoon light with distant haze. Color direction: slate gray, dusty taupe, weathered brown, muted olive, and pale stone tones. Mood: monumental, calm, majestic, and ancient.
```
